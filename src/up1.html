The UP1

Bring it to Life

Building a robot is hard. Trust us, it's just hard, we've been working on it for a while. Programming a robot is fun but building it, well it often involves a lot of swearing.

The UP1 was created so that you could skip that first part and get on to the tinkering. It's ready to go out of the box, you just need to tell it what to do.

Whether you write your own robotics application, or download a package from a selection of pre built behaviors, what you do with the UP1 is up to you.

UP1 Assemble!

One robot, many configurations. Want to run your updroid with spider legs? Two arms instead of one? No prob, swap it out.

CPU

Every robot needs a brain, at least a smart one does. The UP1 cpu plugs into the back of the base and enables the UP1 to carry out its tasks. The first iteration will have wifi, a battery indicator, 3 axis accelerometer, and a gyroscope.

Base

The foundation upon which the UP1 comes together, the base houses the internals and offers mobility with its 3 wheel setup. Future iterations will offer different options for movement.

Manipulator

It's an arm. It manipulates the world around it. Sounds simple but its a pretty big deal. Without it, the updroid would just be driving around taking notes.

Sensors

It sees, it hears, it speaks. The I/O snaps on top of the base and gives the UP1 the senses it needs to perceive the world around it. It's core components are two front facing cameras, a speaker, and a microphone for voice detection.
