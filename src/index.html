Robotics for Everyone

The UP1-Series is our answer to building and programing robots for the average person. It's our first robot which will emphasize ease of use, versatility, and scalability.

The UP1-Series will traverse terrain on wheels. It has sensors through which it can detect its environment and it features two arms which it can use to manipulate it. These arms can be swapped out with different modules to enable different types of interactions with the outside world. A growing number of modules will not only add to the functionality of the UP1-Series but open up different avenues of development as well.

Out of the box, the UP1-Series will have preprogrammed actions and applications. And on top of that, it comes with a software platform that gives you the power of easy programmability and full customization.

The programming environment for robotics has never been an easy one to jump into. The tools are complex and require lots of legwork in order to even get the robot ready to be programmed. The UP1-Series and all future UpDroid models will strip away all that complexity and let you build, tinker, and create completely unhindered.
